Part One

Cold Kitchen (Garde Manger)

The Cold Kitchen or Garde Manger is the section responsible for preparing all cold dishes—from salads to hors d'oeuvres.

What is the Cold Kitchen?

Definition & Purpose

The Cold Kitchen or Garde Manger (French for "keeper of the food") is the kitchen department responsible for preparing all cold dishes. This is the first stage of preparation before ingredients are cooked.

This is where salads, appetizers, cold cuts, pâtés, and more are made. It is crucial because cold food is often the first thing a customer sees—so it must look beautiful and taste great.

❄️ Functions of the Cold Kitchen:

✦Preparation of cold appetizers and salads
✦Making dressings, vinaigrettes, and cold sauces
✦Preparing cold cuts, charcuterie, and pâtés
✦Setting up and decorating buffet tables
✦Storing ingredients that require refrigeration
✦Making pickles, relishes, and sandwiches

Part Two

Hot Kitchen (Cuisine Chaude)

The Hot Kitchen is the heart of the kitchen—where hot food is cooked using fire, heat, and various cooking methods.

What is the Hot Kitchen?

Definition & Purpose

The Hot Kitchen or Cuisine Chaude is the main cooking area where heat is used to prepare food. This is where you'll find stoves, ovens, grills, and fryers.

It is the largest department in the kitchen and is responsible for all hot dishes—from soups to grilled meats, from fried food to baked goods.

🔥 Functions of the Hot Kitchen:

✦Cooking hot main courses (entrées)
✦Making soups, sauces, and gravies
✦Grilling meat, fish, and vegetables
✦Frying, sautéing, and boiling
✦Steaming and poaching food
✦Baking bread, pastry, and desserts

Every member of the kitchen has a specific role and responsibility. Get to know each one.

Executive Chef

TOP

Chef de Cuisine / Head Chef

The highest-ranking leader of the entire kitchen. Plans the menu, maintains quality, and manages all chefs and staff.

📋 Menu Planning👨‍💼 Management✅ Quality Control

Sous Chef

2IC

Second-in-Command / Under Chef

The second-in-command. Fills in for the Executive Chef when absent. Provides direction and assists all stations.

🔄 Deputizing📋 Supervision🤝 Coordination

Sauce Chef

Saucier / Chef Saucier

The specialist in making sauces, soups, and gravies. One of the most important chefs because sauce gives life to every dish.

🫙 Sauces🍲 Soups🥘 Gravies

Grilled Chef

Grillardin / Chef Grillardin

The expert in grilling meat, fish, and vegetables. Maintains proper temperature and doneness for every grilled item.

🥩 Grilling🔥 Roasting🌡️ Temperature

Cold Kitchen Chef

Garde Manger / Chef Garde Manger

In charge of the entire cold kitchen department. Makes salads, appetizers, cold cuts, and all cold food items.

🥗 Salads🧊 Cold Prep🍖 Charcuterie

Pastry Chef

Pâtissier / Chef Pâtissier

The expert in making bread, cakes, pastries, and desserts. Requires skill in baking, decorating, and balancing flavors.

🍞 Baking🎂 Pastry🍮 Desserts

Fish Chef

Poissonnier / Chef Poissonnier

The specialist in preparing and cooking fish and seafood. Skilled in filleting, scaling, and cooking fish in various methods.

🐟 Fish Prep🦐 Seafood🔪 Filleting

Kitchen Helper

Commis / Kitchen Assistant

The support system of the entire kitchen. Cleans, watches over ingredients, helps with prep, and ensures smooth operations.

🧹 Cleaning🔪 Prep Work📦 Stocking
